Defer major launches or investments. The annual **Profection** to your 1st house in Gemini, with Mercury as the Time Lord, shifts the year’s focus squarely onto your identity, physical presence, and personal brand. This is a year of self-definition, but the initial phase requires restraint, not aggressive self-promotion.

This is the time to begin laying the groundwork for personal and professional changes. Your physical vitality should improve, and your ability to project a compelling vision will be heightened. However, a significant divergence in directives emerges. This conflict—Mercury’s speed versus Venus’s deliberation—defines your central challenge for 2029. You must innovate without sacrificing core values.

The most critical and volatile period of the year is **Jun 15–Aug 8, 2029**, a temporal storm window where six predictive systems converge. This is a rare, life-altering transit that signals sudden, electric, and potentially disruptive changes in your relationships, value systems, and financial assets. An unexpected romance, a sudden break-up, or a radical shift in your aesthetic preferences are all high-probability outcomes. Given the **Tajaka** Venusian theme, this transit gains immense power, suggesting that whatever changes occur in your personal life will have direct and immediate financial consequences. Health-wise, this period can bring nervous tension; prioritize grounding activities to manage the Uranian electrical charge. During this same window, Saturn’s sextile to your natal Mercury (**May 22–Jul 16**) provides the mental discipline to navigate the chaos, allowing for sober assessment and structured communication amidst the upheaval.

Following the summer’s intensity, a second Jupiter trine to your Ascendant from **Jul 31 to Sep 7, 2029**, provides an opportunity to integrate the recent shifts. This is a favorable time for solidifying a new public image or personal brand that reflects the profound internal changes. However, a caution flag is raised as Jupiter squares your natal Sun again (**Aug 24–Sep 25**), re-testing your confidence. Avoid arrogance; instead, use this energy to champion the new version of yourself with humble authority. The final quarter continues the theme of structural adjustment. Saturn’s supportive sextile to your MC (**Sep 22–Dec 22**) aids in stabilizing your career path after the year’s volatility. By December, Jupiter’s trine to your natal Mercury (**Dec 11, 2029–Jan 14, 2030**) brings clarity and intellectual breakthroughs, allowing you to articulate the lessons of the year with precision.

**The one action 2029 demands:** Between **Apr 28–Aug 26, 2029**, during the Uranus-Venus conjunction, you must consciously audit and update your core values, both personal and financial, rather than letting external events force your hand. Proactively redefine what brings you pleasure and security.
